Individual Transform Properties

To add animations or transitions to your website, utilizing the CSS transform property is an effective approach. However, a drawback arises when you need to modify a single transform property later; you are required to restate the entire definition for all properties.

Before:
                    transform: translateX(0%) rotate(0deg) scale(0.9);
Now:
                    translateX(0%);
                    rotate(0deg);
                    scale(0.9);
